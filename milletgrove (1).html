Our Story

Modern health rooted in ancient tradition

🌾

Born from the Fields of Tamil Nadu

Millet Grove was born out of a simple observation — the superfoods our ancestors thrived on had been forgotten. Millets that fed generations of Tamil Nadu families were being replaced by processed, nutrient-poor alternatives.

We set out to change that. Starting with a single farm in Tirunelveli in 2022, we began reviving traditional millet cultivation — the same way it was done for centuries, without chemicals, without shortcuts.

🤝

Farmer Partnerships That Matter

Today we work with over 120 small-scale farmers across Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, and Karnataka. We pay fair prices, provide organic inputs, and help farmers transition away from chemical farming.

Every purchase you make directly supports a farming family. We believe that good food and fair trade can go hand in hand.

💚

Our Commitment to You

Every batch of millet that leaves our processing center is tested in our FSSAI-certified lab for pesticide residues, heavy metals, and nutritional content. You deserve to know exactly what you're putting on your family's table.

We're not just selling grain — we're building a community of people who care about where their food comes from and what it does for their bodies.
